About Us

Caspian Grill is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving authentic Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine in Kennesaw, Georgia. Caspian Grill Restaurant is acclaimed for their authentic Mediterranean/Middle Eastern cuisine, warm hospitality and lively ambiance. Caspian Grill provides traditional recipes that contain robust flavors of the Mediterranean region prepared with the freshest ingredients obtained locally.Caspian Grill strives to provide the Marietta community with healthy, fresh, and nutritional Middle Eastern Cuisine in a relaxed and casual dining atmosphere. Our menu features many traditional staples of the Mediterranean Diet, as well as offering original dishes with recipes that have stemmed down from generation to generation at the best value in town.
